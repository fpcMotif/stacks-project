The two definitions

Presheaf 006E $\mathcal F\colon U\mapsto\mathcal F(U)$ with restriction $\rho^U_V\colon\mathcal F(U)\to\mathcal F(V)$ for $V\subseteq U$; $\ \rho^U_U=\mathrm{id}$, $\ \rho^V_W\rho^U_V=\rho^U_W$. A contravariant functor on opens. $s|_V:=\rho^U_V(s)$.
Sheaf 006T Presheaf $+$ for every cover $U=\bigcup U_i$ and compatible $s_i$ ($s_i|_{U_i\cap U_j}=s_j|_{U_i\cap U_j}$), a unique $s$ with $s|_{U_i}=s_i$. Equivalently exactness of $$\mathcal F(U)\;\longrightarrow\;\prod_i\mathcal F(U_i)\;\rightrightarrows\;\prod_{i,j}\mathcal F(U_i\cap U_j).$$

Stalks & germs 0078

Stalk recipe $\mathcal F_x=\operatorname*{colim}_{x\in U}\mathcal F(U)$, filtered colimit over nbhds (reverse inclusion). Germ $s_x$: $(U,s)\sim(U',s')$ iff $s|_{U''}=s'|_{U''}$ on some $U''\ni x$.
  • The identity to memorize: $\ \mathcal O_{\operatorname{Spec} R,\,\mathfrak p}=\operatorname*{colim}_{f\notin\mathfrak p}R_f=R_{\mathfrak p}$. Stalk $=$ localization (01HV).
  • All stalks local $\Rightarrow$ locally ringed space; value at $x$ lives in $\kappa(x)=R_{\mathfrak p}/\mathfrak pR_{\mathfrak p}$.
  • Sheaf $\Rightarrow$ $\mathcal F(U)\hookrightarrow\prod_{x}\mathcal F_x$ (section $=$ its germs).

Sheaves on a basis 009H

  • $D(f)$ form a basis closed under $\cap$ ($D(f)\cap D(g)=D(fg)$).
  • Presheaf on $\mathcal B$ 009I carries less data than on all opens; sheaf-on-$\mathcal B$ axiom 009J.
  • Quasi-compactness $\Rightarrow$ check only finite covers (cofinal, 009K).
  • Extend-off-basis 009N: a sheaf on $\mathcal B$ extends to a unique sheaf on $X$; $\operatorname{Sh}(X)\simeq\operatorname{Sh}(\mathcal B)$ (009O); stalks unchanged.
